Impact

Manufacturing outcomes you can track on every line

BMS Accelerator connects clear standards, daily routines and simple digital signals so you can see whether your plants are moving in the right direction — not just running workshops.

  • +5–15% OEE through structured loss analysis and AM/PM integration.
  • -5–15% unplanned downtime by stabilising basic conditions and clarifying escalation.
  • -10–25% minor stops & speed losses via operator-owned standards and checks.
  • Fewer chronic quality issues through visible parameters and simple problem solving.
  • Improved safety & 5S as a natural side-effect of true equipment ownership.
  • Higher engagement as teams see their actions in the trend, not just in a slide deck.
Method

What is BMS Accelerator?

A structured BMS deployment model that blends classic TPM with digital-ready design. Everything is built so it works on whiteboards first — and connects to apps and dashboards when you’re ready.

  • A clear deployment roadmap from model line to full network.
  • Standardised templates & training content ready to brand for your company.
  • Module-based design for AM, PM, FI, Performance Management and Leadership.
  • Loss & KPI framework that can feed existing OEE, BI or CMMS tools.
  • Digital-friendly, offline-capable — works on paper, whiteboards or devices.

Digitalisation

Use data to see if BMS is really working

BMS Accelerator is designed for a digital world: you can start with markers and magnets, then plug the same logic into BI dashboards or OEE tools.

We keep the data model simple: a small, standard set of losses and KPIs that every plant can understand. That drives cleaner dashboards, easier comparisons and faster decisions at daily, weekly and monthly levels.

  • Standard loss model that feeds OEE and downtime analytics.
  • Clear link between daily boards and digital trends.
  • Ready to connect to Power BI, Tableau or your existing tools.
  • Sensor and IoT-friendly — but not dependent on them.

Deployment

How we deploy BMS Accelerator with your teams

A simple, repeatable pattern that can be adapted to a single pilot site or a global network. Your people stay in the centre — tools and digitalisation support them, not replace them.

  1. Assess & align – understand maturity, losses, strategy and constraints.
  2. Design the roadmap – pick model lines, define waves, set expectations.
  3. Build & adapt the toolkit – brand materials, agree on standards and KPIs.
  4. Deploy & coach – on-the-floor support, routines and leader standard work.
  5. Scale & sustain – grow internal capability, run health checks, refresh as needed.
